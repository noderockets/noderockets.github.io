Mission Objectives

Here is your mission, if choose to accept it

The main objective of your team is to build a javascript powered rocket that once launched will return safely to earth. Using a 2-liter soda bottle as the engine for your rocket, it will use pressurized water and air as it's propellent and can reach heights of several hundred feet.

Onboard the rocket (and preferrably in a seperate and dry compartment) is the command module. The command module is made up of a Raspberry Pi running NodeJS, a 6-axis motion sensor, a temperature sensor, an altimeter and a servo. Using the hardware provided and the data stream from the sensors, you must figure out a strategy for depolying the parachute so that the rocket will safely land.

Your Team

Mission Commander

Somebody has to be in charge of this mission and that's you. You must organize the team and the tasks that need to be performed for a successful mission.

Ground Control

Come launch time, its go time for you. Your job is make sure everything on the ground goes smoothly during launch. Of course, you have to figure out how to make the launch pad work too.

Flight Engineer

Aerodynamics is your speciality. This spacecraft must fly and your job is to design a build a flightworthy craft. Oh yeah, don't forget to attach the parachute.

Communication Specialist

You've got the easy job. All you have to do is make sure the parachute deploys when it's suppossed to. Oh yeah, the president called and wants some kindof fancy dashboard to read the data.
