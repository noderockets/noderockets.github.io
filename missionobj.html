Mission Objectives

Here is your mission, if choose to accept it

The main objective of your team is to build a javascript powered rocket that once launched will record and use telemetry data to safely return to earth ready for it’s next mission. Sounds easy enough right? Your rocket will consist of the following components:

  • Engine
  • Payload Compartment
  • Command Module
  • Parachute and Deployment Cover
  • Fins
  • Nose Cone
  • Launcher Platform

The engine is made from a 2-liter soda bottle and uses water and pressurized air as a propellent. The payload compartment is also made from a 2-liter soda bottle and will house the command module. The command module is the computer system for the rocket. At its core is a Raspberry Pi running NodeJS. It also contains several sensors that expose their data via a node module. Attached to the Pi is a servo which will be used to deploy the parachute. The parachute is the simplest component, but may be the most important. Using the data from the sensors you can create a strategy for deploying the chute. The fins, nose cone and overall aerodynamic design are critical for achieving the optimal flight and highest altitude.

The launch platform consists of 3 valves for controlling the air, water and launch of the rocket. It also has a sensor for calculating the air pressure of the system. The launch platform exposes an API for controlling the valves and reading the pressure. You will need to use this API to control the different stages of launch.

In addition to the main rocket components, their is additional hardware (camera, led lights, gamepad, etc.) that you can integrate with the command module or launch platform. It will be your teams job to engineer and incorporate at least one additional item as part of your design. Your rocket will compete with other rockets for it’s ability to be reusable, reach high altitude and also creativity.

  • Successfully Launch Rocket
  • Deploy Parachute
  • Incorporate Specialized Mission Hardware

Below you will find the critical roles that your team needs to complete this mission. Each role has detailed instructions on how to complete its job. You may have multiple people fill each role or have somebody fill multiple roles. It's up to you, good luck!

Your Team

Mission Commander

Somebody has to be in charge of this mission and that's you. You must organize the team and the tasks that need to be performed for a successful mission.

Ground Control

Come launch time, its go time for you. Your job is to control each stage of the launch and not blow anything up.

Flight Engineer

Aerodynamics is your speciality. This spacecraft must fly and your job is to design and build a flightworthy craft. Oh yeah, don't forget to attach the parachute.

Communication Specialist

You've got the easy job. All you have to do is make sure the parachute deploys when it's suppossed to. Oh yeah, the president called and wants some kindof fancy dashboard to see the data.
